Classrooms and Seminar Halls

We aim to provide the best infrastructure to our students. Our class rooms and seminar halls are well-furnished with comfortable seating arrangement and good acoustics. These halls have teaching aids like LCD projectors, screens & audio visual and multimedia facilities for presentations, discussions and making analysis. We have a Stationery Centre and a Departmental Store to fulfil the daily requirements of our students.

We guarantee an environment that is intellectually stimulating and physically invigorating. There is no compromise when it comes to adopting a professional approach to education. The faculty is a pick of the finest, highly qualified and committed academicians in every field.

Library

A college library is like a time-machine that takes our students to various technological eras and the far-history of human behavioural patterns. Our library is spacious, well-ventilated and has a large seating capacity. The library has a rich collection of technical and soft skill books. We also have autobiographies of entrepreneurs, motivational books, journals, research papers and e-journals too. This saves our students the effort to collect study material from various sources. Supportive facilities like printouts, photocopies, etc. are also available at our library.

Canteen & Cafeteria

A variety of hygienic and nutritious food items are available in the college canteen. The canteen provides onsite dining, food and beverage vending, a relaxed ambience and follows highest standards for food quality. The canteen plays host to several interesting conversations between students and faculty on a regular basis. It is an informal environment that encourages open conversation, and promotes the overall culture of innovation and creativity on campus.

Computer Centre

The Computer Centre consists of technically advanced systems that fulfil all the programming, learning and innovative needs of the students and faculty. It is air-conditioned, and well equipped and the computers have the latest software solutions with printing facility. Mounted LCD projectors are available for instructional classes.

WIFI Facility

The College campus is completely wifi connected. Even Classrooms, Labs , Hostel, Canteen and Library have fast broadband wifi coverage.
